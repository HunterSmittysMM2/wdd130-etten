No Bake Cookies

    Recipe Info:
    Prep Time: 10 mins
    Cook Time: 5 mins
    Additional Time: 30 mins
    Total Time: 45 mins
    Servings: 36
    Yield: 3 dozen

    Ingredients
  • 1 ¾ cups white sugar (can use natural sweeteners)
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup milk (can use evaporated milk, or condensed milk, or half and half, or heavy cream)
  • 4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder (can use hot cocoa mix)
  • ½ cup crunchy peanut butter (can use granola butter)
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (can use maple syrup or honey)
  • waxed paper
    Directions
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar, butter, milk, and cocoa in a medium saucepan; bring to a boil, stirring often. Boil for exactly 1 ½ minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, and stir in peanut butter.
  4. Add oats and vanilla; stir until well combined.
  5. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper.
  6. Let cool until hardened. Enjoy!
    Recipe Info:
    Prep Time: 10 mins
    Cook Time: 10 mins
    Total Time: 20 mins
    Servings: 24
    Yield: 24 dozen

    Ingredients
  • 1 ½ cups brown sugar (can use white sugar, honey, or maple syrup)
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs (can use mashed bananas, apple sauce, or yogurt)
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips (can use raisins)
    Directions
  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Beat brown sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffier in texture. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Gradually stir in flour until well combined; fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Cool cookies on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks.
