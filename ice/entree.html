Pork Tacos (Entree 1)

Recipe Info

    Prep Time: 5m Cook Time: 15m Ingredients: 5 Servings: 2 Ingredients
  • tablespoon oil
  • 1 pound boneless pork chops, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 package taco seasoning mix (can substitution with a combo of chili powder and cumin)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • warmed tortillas
    • Instructions Heat oil in large pan on medium heat. Add pork; cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until no longer pink. Stir in seasoning mix and water. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 to 7 minutes or until most of liquid is absorbed, stirring occasionally. Spoon into warm tortillas and serve with desired toppings.

Chicken Parmesan Pasta (Entree 2)

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • 12 oz. cooked frozen breaded chicken cutlets
  • 1 lb. cavatappi pasta (can use any other short pasta)
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil (can use other types of oil, such as vegetable oil)
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped (can also use green onion, onion powder, or minced onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped (can use minced garlic or garlic powder)
  • 4 cups store-bought or marinara sauce
  • 2 oz. Parmesan, finely grated (about 1 cup), divided, plus more for serving
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozzarella
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Torn fresh basil for serving

Directions:

  1. Cook frozen chicken according to package instructions. Let cool, then cut crosswise into strips.
  2. Arrange a rack in center of oven; preheat to 400°. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta, stirring occasionally, according to package directions. Drain.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Cook onion, stirring occasionally, until translucent, about 7 minutes. Add garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, around 1 minute more. Add marinara and 1 tsp. salt and bring to a low simmer.
  4. Add pasta to skillet and stir to coat in sauce. Add 1/2 cup Parmesan and toss until melted. Top with 1/2 cup Parmesan, then sprinkle with mozzarella. Arrange chicken on top of mozzarella.
  5. Bake chicken Parm pasta until cheese is melted and chicken is warmed through, 10 to 12 minutes.
  6. Season with a few grinds of pepper. Top with basil and more Parmesan.
